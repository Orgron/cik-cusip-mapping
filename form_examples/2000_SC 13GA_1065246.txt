         Pursuant to Rule 13d-2(b) of Regulation 13D-G of the General Rules and
Regulations under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the "Act"),

(the "Stock"), of Hi/fn, Inc. (the "Issuer").

previous filing, Idanta Partners Ltd. is referred to as "IPL", Dunn Family
Trust, David J. Dunn, Trustee, are referred to as "DFT" and "DJD," respectively,
and Jonathan Huberman is referred to as "JH".
