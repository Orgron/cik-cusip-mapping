<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: right"><B>Exhibit&nbsp;(a)(1)(U)</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; text-align: left; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0; margin-bottom: 0pt; background-color: white">SPHINX INVESTMENT CORP. ANNOUNCES EXTENSION
OF TENDER OFFER TO PURCHASE ALL OUTSTANDING COMMON SHARES AND ASSOCIATED RIGHTS OF PERFORMANCE SHIPPING INC.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">NEW YORK, July&nbsp;31,
2025 /PRNewswire/ -- Sphinx Investment Corp. (&ldquo;<U>Sphinx</U>&rdquo;) today announced that it has extended the expiration date of
its previously announced offer to purchase all of the issued and outstanding common shares, par value $0.01 per share (the &ldquo;<U>Common
Shares</U>&rdquo;), of Performance Shipping Inc. (&ldquo;<U>Performance</U>&rdquo;) (including the associated preferred stock purchase
rights (the &ldquo;<U>Rights</U>&rdquo;, and together with the Common Shares, the &ldquo;<U>Shares</U>&rdquo;) for $3.00 per Share in
cash, without interest, less any applicable withholding taxes (the &ldquo;<U>Offer</U>&rdquo;).</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white"><B>The expiration date
of the Offer has been extended to 11:59 p.m., New York City time, on February&nbsp;13, 2026.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">Continental Stock
Transfer&nbsp;&amp; Trust Company, the tender offer agent for the Offer, has advised Sphinx that as of 5:00 p.m., New York City
time, on July&nbsp;30, 2025, the last business day prior to the announcement of the extension of the Offer, 2,152,961 Shares had
been validly tendered into the Offer and not validly withdrawn.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify; background-color: white">The Offer is being made
pursuant to the terms and conditions described in the Amended and Restated Offer to Purchase dated October&nbsp;30, 2023, as amended and
