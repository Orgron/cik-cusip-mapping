(less applicable withholding taxes) (such consideration, together with the Share
Consideration, the &#147;<B><I>Transaction Consideration</I></B>&#148;). Parent Group Options are expected to be

<DIV align="center" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 18pt"><B>VOTING AGREEMENT</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>THIS VOTING AGREEMENT </B>(&#147;<U>Agreement</U>&#148;) is entered into as of September&nbsp;20, 2006, by and
between Vitria Technology, Inc., a Delaware corporation (the &#147;<U>Company</U>&#148;), and JoMei Chang
and M. Dale Skeen, individually and as joint tenants (the &#147;<U>Stockholders</U>&#148;).
</DIV>

<DIV align="center" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 18pt"><B>RECITALS</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A.&nbsp;The Stockholders are holders of record and the &#147;beneficial owners&#148; (within the meaning of
Rule&nbsp;13d-3 under the Securities Exchange Act of 1934) of certain shares of common stock of the
Company.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;B.&nbsp;Innovation Technology Group, Inc., a Delaware corporation (&#147;<U>Parent</U>&#148;), ITG
Acquisition, Inc., a Delaware corporation (&#147;<U>Merger Sub</U>&#148;), and the Company are entering into
an Agreement and Plan of Merger of even date herewith (the &#147;<U>Merger Agreement</U>&#148;) which
provides (subject to the conditions set forth therein) for the merger of Merger Sub into the
Company (the &#147;<U>Merger</U>&#148;).
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;C.&nbsp;The Stockholders are entering into this Agreement in order to induce the Company to enter
into the Merger Agreement.
</DIV>

agree as follows:

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B>SECTION 2. RESTRICTIONS ON TRANSFER OF SUBJECT SECURITIES AND VOTING RIGHTS</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>2.1 Restriction on Transfer of Subject Securities</B>. Subject to Section&nbsp;2.3, during the period
from the date of this Agreement through the Expiration Time, the Stockholders, individually and as
joint tenants, agree that they shall not cause or permit any Transfer of any of the Subject
Securities to be effected.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>2.2 Restriction on Transfer of Voting Rights</B>. During the period from the date of this
Agreement through the Expiration Time, the Stockholders, individually and as joint tenants, agree
that they shall ensure that: (a)&nbsp;none of the Subject Securities is deposited into a voting trust;
and (b)&nbsp;no proxy is granted with respect to any of the Subject Securities (other than in connection
with the Stockholders&#146; compliance with Section 3(a) and other than the proxy in the form attached
hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;A delivered to the Company pursuant to Section&nbsp;3(c)), no voting agreement or
similar agreement is entered into with respect to any of the Subject Securities and no power of
attorney is granted with respect to the voting of the Subject Securities.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>2.3 Permitted Transfers</B>. Section&nbsp;2.1 shall not prohibit a Transfer of Subject Securities by
the Stockholders (a)&nbsp;to Parent or to any wholly-owned subsidiary of Parent, (b)&nbsp;to any member of
the Stockholders&#146; immediate family, or to a trust for the benefit of Stockholder or any member of
the Stockholders&#146; immediate family, or (c)&nbsp;upon the death of either of the Stockholders<B>, </B><I>provided,
however</I>, that<B>, </B>in the case of each of clauses &#147;(a)&#148; and &#147;(b)&#148; above, a Transfer referred to in
this sentence shall be permitted only if, as a precondition to such Transfer, the transferee agrees
in writing, reasonably satisfactory in form and substance to the Company, to be bound by all of the
terms of this Agreement and delivers a duly executed proxy in the form attached hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;A
with respect to such transferred Subject Securities.
</DIV>

<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 12pt"><B>SECTION 3. VOTING OF SHARES.</B>
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>(a) </B>The Stockholders, individually and as joint tenants, hereby agree that, prior to the
Expiration Time, at any meeting of the stockholders of the Company, however called, and in any
written action by consent of stockholders of the Company, unless otherwise directed in writing by
the Company, the Stockholders shall cause all issued and outstanding shares of Company Common Stock
Owned by them, individually or as joint tenants, to be voted in favor of: (i)&nbsp;the adoption of the
Merger Agreement<B>; </B>and <B>(</B>ii) the Merger and each of the other transactions contemplated by the Merger
Agreement.
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>(b) </B>Notwithstanding anything to the contrary contained in this Agreement, nothing in this
Agreement (a)&nbsp;limits or affects, or gives rise to any liability of the Stockholders by virtue of,
any actions taken by either one or both of the Stockholders in his or her capacity as an officer or
director of the Company, as applicable, including any actions taken in connection with the exercise
of the rights of the Company or its board of directors (or any committee thereof) under the Merger
Agreement or (b)&nbsp;obligates either one or both of the Stockholders to exercise any option, warrant
or other right to acquire any Company Common Stock.
</DIV>


<DIV align="left" style="font-size: 10pt; margin-top: 6pt">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<B>(c) </B>The Stockholders have delivered to the Company a duly executed proxy in the form attached
hereto as Exhibit&nbsp;A (the &#147;<U>Proxy</U>&#148;), such Proxy covering the Subject Securities. Upon the
execution of this Agreement by the Stockholders, the Stockholders hereby revoke any and all prior
proxies or powers of attorney given by the Stockholders with respect to voting of the Subject
Securities on the matters referred to in Section&nbsp;3(a).
</DIV>
